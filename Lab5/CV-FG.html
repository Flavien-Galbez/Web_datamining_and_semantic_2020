Research projects+/-

Informations
Domain : Machine learning and Clustering (R)
Begin: September 1st, 2019
End: April 6th, 2020
Details
The aim of the project is to estimate vehicle flows and their types from GPS data using unsupervised Machine learning and Clustering methods. This projecttakes place in collaboration with a teacher and a second student. We study different possible solutions by referring to scientific texts already published in the field and trying to find the most adaptable to our problem. Subsequently, this model will be applied to a real sample of data and we will verify its quality. To test the model, we code in R
For now, we have two steps : we classify the different tracks in order to classify the vehicle with the clustered tracks. We used K-means, Gaussian mixture model, Hierarchical and OPTICS clustering to indetify the best clustering methods for our data set. We test the methods with the one day data on the city of Paris.
